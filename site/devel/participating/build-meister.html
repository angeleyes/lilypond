BUILD-MEISTER

The responsibility of the build-meister it is to make sure that binary packages of LilyPond are available for all platforms.

To this end, the build-meister

  • Regularly (say, once a week) packages LilyPond CVS for one platform. This is to check for bugs in the installation procedure.
  • Tries to find people that will build LilyPond packages for other platforms, either for releases or from CVS.
  • Tries to convince vendors (Fedora, Mandrake, etc.) to put lily in their base distributions.
  • Maintains the lilypond.org/download/ page.
