LilyPond in practice

With LilyPond we have tried to make engraving available for everyone, but what does that mean in practice? In other word, what can you do with it?

One of the things you can typeset is pop music. Here is an example: Note how the chords (The "Em7" and "Hm7" printed at the top) use Danish chord names. This is simply a layout option. By switching back a toggle, you can print normal American chord names.
