Introducing LilyPond

``A thing of beauty is a joy forever''
--John Keats

Seven years ago, we set out to capture the rules of music engraving in a program: a machine may be mechanic, but why should its products be? Our quest and our answer are embodied in LilyPond. It prints music beautifully, and we built it like we expect software to be: robust, open and flexible.

Have fun!

Han-Wen Nienhuys & Jan Nieuwenhuizen.
