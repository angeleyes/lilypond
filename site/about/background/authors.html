AUTHORS

This file lists authors of GNU LilyPond, and what they wrote. This list is alphabetically ordered.

  • Tom Cato Amundsen, accordion symbols, some lilypond-book.py
  • Jean-Baptiste Lamy Tablature support
  • Mats Bengtsson, http://www.s3.kth.se/~matsb/ lots of testing, fixes, general comments and contributions.
  • Laura Conrad (lconrad@laymusic.org) fixes to abc2ly
  • Jan Arne Fagertun, TeX titling and lytodvi.sh
  • Anthony Fok, Debian package: debian/*
  • James Hammons, American chord names, gnu-windows testing
  • Bjoern Jacke German glossary stuff.
  • Chris Jackson, Piano pedals, Emacs mode indentation, directed arpeggios.
  • Neil Jerram. Glossary translations.
  • Heikki Junes. Finnish note names, updates to Emacs-mode
  • Michael Krause, breathing signs
  • Dirk Lattermann, minor improvements on accidentals/ties/multi measure rests
  • Werner Lemberg, misc bugfixes, some Beam and Stem code.
  • Adrian Mariano Reference manual, tutorial fixes, glossary.
  • Christian Mondrup Glossary.
  • Han-Wen Nienhuys, http://www.cs.uu.nl/~hanwen/ Main author (initials: HWN).
  • Jan Nieuwenhuizen, http://www.xs4all.nl/~jantien/ Main author (initials: JCN).
  • Alexandre Oliva, http://sunsite.unicamp.br/~oliva/ testing
  • Francois Pinard, http://www.iro.umontreal.ca/~pinard/ parts of Documentation/user/glossary*, started internationalization stuff
  • Graham Percival, http://www.sfu.ca/~gperciva/ minor documentation fixes.
  • Stephen Peters, pdfTeX support
  • Glen Prideaux, minor bug fixes, lyric-phrasing.
  • Roy R. Rankin, major extension, fixes to abc2ly, lilypond bug fixes
  • Juergen Reuter Ancient notation support (custos, porrectus, mensural notation, ancient clefs, etc.)
  • August S.Sigov Russian translation
  • Rune Zedeler Drum notation, beaming and auto-accidental code. Font updates. Miscellaneous fixes.
