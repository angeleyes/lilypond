Donate to LilyPond development

Three good reasons to sponsor LilyPond development:

  • More sponsors means more time for LilyPond.

    Without sponsors, we have to pursue other work to feed ourselves and our families, and will have less time to improve LilyPond.

  • With your support, we can support you.

    With a donation, your platform, your needs, your platform, your wishes, your feature-requests and your bug-reports will get extra attention.

  • Everyone benefits.

    Helping us helps others, since every improvement made for you will be passed on to the rest of the user community.

LilyPond is free/open-source software. Using, copying and changing is and will remain free. If you want LilyPond to stay healthy and active, then sponsor us.
