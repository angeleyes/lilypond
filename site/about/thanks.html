LilyPond was brought to you by

  • Han-Wen Nienhuys and Jan Nieuwenhuizen, core development

    Jan is currently working as a Programmer/Open Source specialist. Jan plays the viola. Han-Wen is setting up a company in LilyPond support. In the remaining time, he tries to master the French Horn.

    Jan and Han-Wen met in a youth orchestra in the Netherlands. During that time, Jan was tinkering with MusicTeX preprocessors, and got Han-Wen interested. When he joined in it was decided that we should start afresh with a completely different approach. What we created is now known as LilyPond.

    About the origin of the name ‘LilyPond’

  • Graham Percival, documentation editor

    Graham is currently studying viola at the University of Victoria, Canada, despite playing viola for two years and cello for twenty. When he's not buried in academic work or LilyPond doc work, he composes tonal music.

  • Pedro Kroeger, Build meister

    Pedro is a professor of composition, harmony and counterpoint at the Federal University of Bahia, Brazil. In his free time he enjoys playing the viola and hacking some code.
