LilyPond is music printing

LilyPond is an "automated engraving system." It formats music beautifully and automatically, and has a friendly syntax for its input files. It is libre software (‘open source’).
  • Never heard of LilyPond? Read the introduction
  • Questions? Read the FAQ
  • Interested in background information? Read our essay on music engraving and computer notation, or look at our publications.
  • LilyPond was written, conceived and directed by the authors. Helping hands are listed in the Acknowledgements
